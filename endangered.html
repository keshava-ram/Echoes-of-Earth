Endangered Animal: Tiger

About the Tiger

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest species among the Felidae and is known for its pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur. Tigers are apex predators and are primarily found in Asia.

Why Are Tigers Endangered?

  • Loss of habitat due to deforestation
  • Illegal poaching for fur and body parts
  • Human-wildlife conflict

Conservation Efforts

Governments and wildlife organizations are working to protect tigers through anti-poaching laws, wildlife sanctuaries, and awareness campaigns.
