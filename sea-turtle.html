Endangered Animal: Sea Turtle

About the Sea Turtle

The sea turtle (Cheloniidae & Dermochelyidae families) is a marine reptile that has existed for over 100 million years. These graceful creatures play a crucial role in marine ecosystems, especially in maintaining healthy seagrass beds and coral reefs.

Why Are Sea Turtles Endangered?

  • Pollution and plastic waste in oceans
  • Illegal poaching for shells, meat, and eggs
  • Climate change affecting nesting sites
  • Bycatch in commercial fishing nets

Conservation Efforts

Organizations and governments protect sea turtles by implementing fishing regulations, beach conservation programs, and global awareness campaigns to reduce plastic waste.
